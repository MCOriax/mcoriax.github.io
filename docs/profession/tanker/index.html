How to level up

Every level takes 100 EXP, and the class caps at level 120. Earn experience by performing the actions below — each is configured in this profession's YAML file. Ranges such as a~b award a random amount between a and b; numeric conditions such as 1-10 match the amount involved (for example damage dealt or survived).

ActionHow it triggersEXP
receive_damageTake damage (by damage received)1-10 → 5~10 EXP
11-50 → 20~40 EXP
51-100 → 60~100 EXP
shield_blockBlock incoming damage with a shield10~18 EXP
dodge_actionEvade incoming damage (chance scales with agility)20 EXP

These values come from the bundled professions/tanker.yml. Server owners can edit them or add entirely new professions — see Professions & EXP.
