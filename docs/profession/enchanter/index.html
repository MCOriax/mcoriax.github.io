How to level up

Every level takes 130 EXP, and the class caps at level 100. Earn experience by performing the actions below — each is configured in this profession's YAML file. Ranges such as a~b award a random amount between a and b; numeric conditions such as 1-10 match the amount involved (for example damage dealt or survived).

ActionHow it triggersEXP
enchant_itemEnchant an item at an enchanting table20~35 EXP
repair_itemTake a repaired or combined item out of an anvil8~15 EXP
brew_potionCollect a brewed potion from a brewing stand12 EXP
consume_potionDrink a potion5~10 EXP
repair_tridentTake a repaired trident out of an anvil12~20 EXP
clean_armor_trimWash a trimmed armor piece in a cauldron (rate-limited)8~12 EXP

These values come from the bundled professions/enchanter.yml. Server owners can edit them or add entirely new professions — see Professions & EXP.
