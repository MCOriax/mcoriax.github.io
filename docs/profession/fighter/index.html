How to level up

Every level takes 150 EXP, and the class caps at level 100. Earn experience by performing the actions below — each is configured in this profession's YAML file. Ranges such as a~b award a random amount between a and b; numeric conditions such as 1-10 match the amount involved (for example damage dealt or survived).

ActionHow it triggersEXP
deal_damageDeal melee damage to another entity (by damage dealt)1-10 → 2~5 EXP

Health regenerates automatically by 1% of maximum health every 5 seconds. Food governs the hunger bar only.
